About LCEEP

The Laboratory of Energy Conversion and Power Electronics (LCEEP) is a research center dedicated to the study and development of renewable energy technologies, with special emphasis on wind energy and energy conversion systems.

Our mission is to contribute to sustainable development through scientific research, training highly qualified professionals and knowledge transfer to industry and society.

Research Areas

  • Wind energy conversion systems
  • Power electronics for renewable energies
  • Microgrids and energy storage systems
  • Predictive control of power converters
  • Integration of renewable energies to the electrical grid
  • Smart cities and energy efficiency
