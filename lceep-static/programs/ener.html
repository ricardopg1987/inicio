ENER Program

Maule Sustainable Energy Congress

ENER is the Maule Sustainable Energy Congress, one of Chile's most important scientific events in the field of renewable energy and energy sustainability.

About the Congress

The ENER Congress has established itself as a fundamental platform for knowledge exchange among academics, researchers, students, companies and professionals in the energy sector. Since its inception, the event has gathered over 100 scientists per edition presenting the latest advances in research and development of clean energy technologies.

This biennial congress takes place in the Maule Region, Chile, and has grown to become a national reference in the discussion of innovative solutions for the country's and region's energy challenges.

Congress Objectives

  • Promote scientific research in renewable energy and sustainability
  • Facilitate collaboration between universities, research centers and industry
  • Disseminate research results and innovative technological developments
  • Foster the formation of national and international collaboration networks
  • Contribute to technology transfer to the productive sector
  • Strengthen the training of advanced human capital in sustainable energy

Thematic Areas

The congress addresses various areas related to sustainable energy:

  • Solar Energy: Photovoltaic systems, solar concentration, emerging technologies
  • Wind Energy: Wind turbines, wind farms, grid integration
  • Power Electronics: Converters, inverters, system control
  • Microgrids: Design, management and control of smart microgrids
  • Energy Storage: Batteries, supercapacitors, hydrogen
  • Smart Cities: Urban energy efficiency, smart buildings
  • Renewable Energy Integration: Power quality, grid stability
  • Energy Policies: Regulatory framework, incentives, economic analysis

Featured Editions

ENER18 (2018) - Sixth Edition

The sixth edition of the Congress gathered more than 100 scientists who presented the latest advances in energy. In parallel, Expo Maule Energy was held, a fair showcasing innovative equipment and prototypes for the energy sector.

ENER16 (2016) - Fourth Edition

The Fourth Renewable Energy Symposium brought together experts from around the world to discuss innovative solutions in renewable energy utilization, microgrids and smart cities, establishing itself as one of the main national scientific meetings.

Participation

The congress is aimed at researchers, academics, graduate students, industry professionals and authorities related to the energy sector. Participants can present research papers in oral or poster format, participate in roundtable discussions and attend plenary lectures by invited experts.

Organization

The ENER Congress is organized by the Laboratory of Energy Conversion and Power Electronics (LCEEP) at Universidad de Talca, in collaboration with other academic institutions and national and international research centers.

Contact Information

For more information about future editions of the ENER Congress, send an email to ener@lceep.cl or visit our website.
