About Our Programs

LCEEP coordinates and participates in various academic and research programs that promote the development of sustainable energy in Chile and worldwide. These programs facilitate collaboration between institutions, knowledge exchange and advanced human capital formation.

Through congresses, webinars and conferences, we connect researchers, students and industry professionals to address current and future challenges in the energy sector.

Objectives of Our Programs

  • Promote the dissemination of scientific knowledge in renewable energy
  • Facilitate collaboration between academic institutions and industry
  • Foster training of students and young researchers
  • Create international networks for scientific cooperation
  • Transfer technology and knowledge to the productive sector
  • Contribute to sustainable development and energy transition
