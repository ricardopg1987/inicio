SERC Distinguished Lecture Program

Solar Energy Research Center - FONDAP SERC Chile

The SERC Distinguished Lecture Program is an initiative of the Solar Energy Research Center (SERC Chile) that invites renowned international experts to share their knowledge in solar energy and related technologies with the Chilean scientific community.

About SERC Chile

SERC Chile is a research center of excellence funded by the Fund for Advanced Research in Priority Areas (FONDAP) of the National Agency for Research and Development (ANID). Its mission is to develop world-class science in solar energy and related technologies to contribute to Chile's energy transition.

The center brings together leading researchers from various Chilean institutions, working collaboratively in areas such as photovoltaic solar energy, solar thermal, energy storage, power electronics and integrated energy systems.

Program Objectives

  • Bring world leaders in solar energy research to Chile
  • Facilitate knowledge exchange between international experts and the local community
  • Inspire students and young researchers with role models of excellence
  • Promote international collaborations in research projects
  • Disseminate the state of the art in solar technologies and renewable energy
  • Strengthen SERC Chile's visibility in the international scientific community

Lecture Format

The Distinguished Lecture Program organizes visits from outstanding international researchers who conduct:

  • Keynote Lectures: Presentations on frontier research
  • Technical Seminars: Specialized talks for researchers
  • Meetings with Researchers: Work and discussion sessions
  • Laboratory Visits: Direct interaction with research facilities
  • Student Sessions: Inspirational meetings for human capital formation

Thematic Areas

The lectures cover various areas related to solar energy:

  • Photovoltaic technology: silicon cells, thin films, perovskites
  • Solar thermal energy and solar concentration
  • Thermal and electrical energy storage
  • Power electronics for solar systems
  • Hybrid systems and solar microgrids
  • Solar hydrogen and solar fuels
  • Building-integrated photovoltaic systems
  • Modeling and simulation of solar systems
  • Solar energy economics and policies

Featured Speakers

The program has hosted renowned experts from world-leading institutions, including professors from universities such as MIT, Stanford, TU Delft, NREL, Fraunhofer ISE, among others. These experts are selected for their outstanding trajectory and significant contributions to the field of solar energy.

Program Impact

Since its inception, the Distinguished Lecture Program has:

  • Brought over 30 top-level international experts to Chile
  • Facilitated the initiation of international research collaborations
  • Inspired hundreds of students and young researchers
  • Contributed to the international visibility of Chilean research
  • Generated opportunities for internships and academic mobility

LCEEP's Participation

LCEEP is an active part of the SERC Chile ecosystem, collaborating in research on power electronics for photovoltaic systems, energy storage and solar microgrids. As an associated institution, LCEEP participates in organizing distinguished lectures when they are held in the Maule Region and facilitates interaction of its researchers with visiting experts.

Program Venues

Lectures are held at the different institutions that make up SERC Chile, including:

  • Universidad de Chile (Santiago)
  • Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile (Santiago)
  • Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María (Valparaíso)
  • Universidad de Talca (Curicó) - LCEEP
  • Universidad de Concepción (Concepción)

More Information

To learn about upcoming distinguished lectures and SERC Chile activities, visit www.serc.cl or contact LCEEP at info@lceep.cl for information about lectures in the Maule Region.
